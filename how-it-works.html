What’s usually inside

Exact SKUs rotate monthly. The mix stays mood-true.

  • 3–4 crunch items

    Chips, crackers, pretzels — couch-proof staples.

  • 3–4 sweet treats

    Candy, cookies, chocolate — the intermission energy.

  • 1 snack card

    Theme notes, pairing ideas, and what’s new this month.

FAQ

Straight answers for founding members.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Skip or cancel anytime before your next ship date. No guilt, no gotchas.

Do you ship nationally?

Founding drop targets the contiguous US. Shipping is free on founding orders in this mockup.

Are snacks allergen-labeled?

Yes — each box includes allergen callouts on the snack card. Always check individual packaging.

Is this a cannabis brand?

No. Mellow Munch is a food-forward snack lifestyle brand. Relaxed vibes, zero controlled-substance messaging.
